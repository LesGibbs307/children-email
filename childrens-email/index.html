Cape Day 2018

Our patients may not be able to leap buildings in a single bound but they have been known to fight off some pretty formidable foes, taking on illness and injury with strength, grace and courage. Our heroes embrace their superpowers and face tough challenges head on. Sometimes, they even wear capes.

For our fifth annual Cape Day on Friday, Oct. 19, we invite you to show your support for these small but mighty superheroes and to honor the many members of our community—from teachers and firemen to moms and dads—who inspire kids to keep up the fight.
